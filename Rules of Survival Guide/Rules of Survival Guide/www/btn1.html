Rules of Survival Guide: Tips and Tricks for the Beginners

 

Rules  Survival is a PUBG battle royale game. There are 120 survivors in the game, who must eliminate one another until a sole survivor remains. To become the final survivor in Rules of Survival, you will need a decent strategy, tips, and practice. For this guide, we’ll cover basic tips. These tips are for finding wea pons, eliminating opponents, and becoming the last player standing in Rules of Survival.

Rules Survival Guide: Tips and Tricks

Tip 1: Know the Modes (Solo, Duo, and Squad)

First, keep in mind that there are multiple game modes to choose from. It depends on whether you want to play alone or you like to play with other players in a team.

There’s Solo mode. This is essentially a huge free-for-all with every survivor against one another.

Then there are the Duo and Squad. These modes place you on a team with either one or three other players, respectively.

We suggest starting out in Solo unless you have friends to play Duo or Squad with. The problem with playing the team-based modes with random players is that most of these players play without care for their teammates. Random players like to go do their own thing. So this can be quite frustrating.

It is better if you are with your friends in the game. This is an advantage in Duos and Squads over those who team up with random players.

Tip 2: Choose the Best and Easy Places to Drop

Just like in PUBG, each round in Rules Survival starts with survivors parachuting into the island arena via aircraft. It’s important to choose your drop point carefully, as your landing location can determine the type of loot you’re likely to find. A good rule of thumb is to land in areas with a lot of buildings since buildings usually contain a variety of loot including weapons, ammo, and other items.

Tip 3: Watch Out  Where Others Players Land

When you are parachuting into the map, look around and take note of where enemy players are landing. This will help you anticipate from which direction opponents may come from once you hit the ground. You can also place a marker on the map by tapping the minimap and tapping again to place a marker at a specific location. Watching where your opponents land allows you to eliminate them quickly and pick up their loot.

Parachute in quickly to get the best loot early on in Rules of Survival.

Tip 4: Land Earlier than Other players and Hunker Down

The most important thing in the game is to try to land earlier. So you can find the more valuable weapons for your survival.

In addition, landing early can give you time to hunker down and wait for other players to approach. This will allow you to pick off your opponents before moving on.

Tip 5: Circular Reasoning

A feature of Rules Survival is the circles that gradually drive players toward a location by damaging players outside the safe zone. There is a small circle appearing on the map inside a slightly larger circle. You have to reach the smaller circle before the larger one encroaches on its border to avoid taking damage.

It’s natural to want to avoid taking damage at all costs. However, early on in the match, it is very easy to recover from circle damage. It’s later when you’ll start to encounter more players that circle damage can become more of an issue.

Pay attention to the circles, and use them to your advantage. What you can do is hang out in the danger zone for a bit before the damage you sustain becomes problematic. There’s a good chance you’ll spot opponents trying to run from the outer circle to the safe zone along the way. This is the perfect opportunity to pick them off.

Air-dropped loot crates can be used to bait other players in Rules of Survival.

Tip 6: Bait with Crates

As you play through a round of Rules Survival, you’ll occasionally see planes drop off crates full of goodies. These often contain valuable weapons and items which can be quite enticing. But do not instinctively run toward every crate you see. Hang back for a bit and watch the crate. Eventually, other players will spot the crate and try to approach it. This will give you the perfect opportunity to eliminate them. When you’re ready to move on (perhaps when the circles are moving in), go ahead and steal the crate, as well as all the goodies, dropped by the players you eliminated. Other players will use this tactic as well, so make sure to approach crates, dead opponents, and other loot with caution.

Tip 7: Move Between Cover

Like in most multiplayer arena shooters, the longer you’re out in the open, the more likely you are to get shot by an opponent. This definitely applies to Rules of Survival too. So try to avoid being outside in the open for too long. When you decide to head across the terrain, stay behind cover and move between cover. This is to decrease your chances of being spotted by a sniper or another opponent.

If you follow these tips, you should have much better odds of becoming the last player standing in Rules of Survival.
