The Ultimate Rules of Survival Gun Guide | Get shooting!

 

Here, we give you the ultimate Rules of Survival Gun Guide to help you choose guns in a snap. Because, yes, every second matters in the battlefield.

The controls take a bit of getting used to at first, but once you’ve get the hang of it, you’d be shooting like crazy or sniping like a real one-shot man. And now that you know how to play the game with your skills and strategy, its time to learn your best friend in the battlefield: your guns.

Assault Rifles

Assault Rifles are one of the easy choices for players. Why? Easier to manage, good damage, good magazine capacity and very customizable with its wide range of attachments available.

– Muzzle (Rifle Silencer, Rifle Compensator, Rifle Flash Hider)
– Magazines (Rifle QD-Mag, Rifle Ex-Mag, Rifle-Ex-QD-Mag)
– Scopes (Red Dot Sight, Holo-Sight, 2x Scope, 4x Scope, 8x Scope)

Sniper Rifles

One could think these as campers’ favorite rifle, its probably true. But snipers aren’t only for campers. Grab a decent gun for mid range shootings and a sniper for the other and you could be already having a deadly combination.

AWM

One of the most popular guns, AWM comes with a built-in 8x scope. Attaching a different scope on it cancels out the 8x scope and replaces it with the attachment effect.

Damage: 84
Ammo: SR Ammo
Mag. Capacity: 5
Attachments: 4
– Muzzle (SR Silencer, SR Compensator, SR Flash Hider)
– Magazines (Rifle QD-Mag, Rifle Ex-Mag, Rifle Ex-QD-Mag)
– Scopes (Red Dot Sight, Holo-Sight, 2x Scope, 4x Scope, 8x Scope)
– Cheek Pad (SR Cheek Pad)

SVD

Not much damage but has been one of players’ favorites with its decent mag capacity and easier manageability. Lets you fire those quick long ranged shots!

Damage: 33
Ammo: SR Ammo
Mag. Capacity: 10
Attachments:
– Muzzle (Rifle Silencer, Rifle Compensator, Rifle Flash Hider)
– Magazines (Rifle QD-Mag, Rifle Ex-Mag, Rifle Ex-QD-Mag)
– Scopes (Red Dot Sight, Holo-Sight, 2x Scope, 4x Scope, 8x Scope)

Barrett

Sniper Rifle with the highest damage. Found in supply crates. Has very high damage and accuracy.

Damage: 100+
Ammo: SR Ammo
Mag. Capacity: 5
Attachments: 4
– Muzzle (SR Silencer, SR Compensator, SR Flash Hider)
– Magazines (Rifle QD-Mag, Rifle Ex-Mag, Rifle Ex-QD-Mag)
– Scopes (Red Dot Sight, Holo-Sight, 2x Scope, 4x Scope, 8x Scope)
– Stock (SR Cheek Pad)

AS VAL

Hard to master with its very random damage. Its like playing dice with your enemy.

Damage: Very Random
Ammo: SR Ammo
Mag. Capacity: 20
Attachments: 4
– Muzzle (Silencer – AS VAL comes with a default silencer and cannot be removed or replaced)
– Magazines (SR QD-Mag, SR Ex-Mag, SR Ex-QD-Mag)
– Scopes (4x scope – AS VAL comes with a default 4x scope and cannot be removed or replaced)
– Stock (SR Cheek Pad)

Shotguns
